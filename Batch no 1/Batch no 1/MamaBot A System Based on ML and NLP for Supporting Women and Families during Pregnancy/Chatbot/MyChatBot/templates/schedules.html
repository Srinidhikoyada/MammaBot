Pregnancy Month-wise Schedule

Month 1

  • Start taking prenatal vitamins with folic acid.
  • Avoid alcohol, smoking, and caffeine.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and lean proteins.
  • Schedule your first prenatal appointment.

Month 2

  • Continue taking prenatal vitamins.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water.
  • Start documenting your pregnancy journey.
  • Rest as much as possible to combat fatigue.

Month 3

  • Consider telling close family and friends about your pregnancy.
  • Start wearing comfortable clothing as your body changes.
  • Focus on eating small, frequent meals to manage nausea.
  • Attend your first ultrasound appointment.

Month 4

  • Begin light exercise routines approved by your doctor.
  • Continue regular prenatal checkups.
  • Look into maternity leave options at work.
  • Start planning for your babyâ€™s nursery.

Month 5

  • Consider taking a prenatal yoga class.
  • Begin a baby registry for essential items.
  • Stay informed about pregnancy and childbirth.
  • Discuss any concerns with your healthcare provider.

Month 6

  • Monitor your baby's movements and patterns.
  • Attend childbirth education classes.
  • Stay active with gentle exercises.
  • Prepare for your glucose screening test.

Month 7

  • Prepare for your baby shower.
  • Plan your hospital bag essentials.
  • Discuss your birth plan with your doctor.
  • Continue attending prenatal appointments.

Month 8

  • Finish setting up the nursery.
  • Install your car seat correctly.
  • Take care of yourself with relaxation techniques.
  • Consider breastfeeding classes.

Month 9

  • Finalize your birth plan.
  • Keep a list of important contacts handy.
  • Prepare meals to freeze for after the baby arrives.
  • Rest and conserve energy for labor.

Month 10

  • Stay calm and prepare for labor.
  • Continue attending your weekly prenatal checkups.
  • Ensure your hospital bag is ready to go.
  • Communicate with your partner and support team.
